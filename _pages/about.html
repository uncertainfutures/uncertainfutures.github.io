This website was created as a means of synthesizing reference materials about theory, software tools, and case study materials for decision making in the face of multiple plausible futures, with a focus on water modelling and management.

We welcome contributions and comments on GitHub.

Initial development of this website was funded by the Queensland Water Modelling Network Small Grants Program 2020 in recognition of the importance of the issue of communication of scenario results and the need for work to adapt decision frameworks to local use cases and increase their adoption.

The materials are provided under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0). We encourage the use of both theoretical and case study resources to showcase modelling to end user communication good practice that cover decision making, model outputs, constraints and risks, and encourage critical thinking by including them as resources in training.

We recognise the support of:
